Principles

While I employ many strategies in my work, these are my core design values.

Collaboration, not compromise

Strong opinions, loosely held. The best solutions take many voices to craft, so let's challenge assumptions together.

Inclusion from the beginning

People aren't edge cases. Accessibility and inclusion are essential before, during, and after design.

Articulate the ROI of design

Explaining the value of design to stakeholders isn't easy, but it's vital to the success of a product. Measure outcomes and get scrappy.

Learn and teach

How to keep design relevant in a fast-changing world: continuous learning to strengthen my skills, and continuous teaching to strengthen my team and peers.
